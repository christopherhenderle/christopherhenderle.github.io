A Puzzle/RPG hybrid. Think Puzzle Quest.

After the successful completion of My Spanish/French Coach, two coworkers began development of the prototype to this game. Upon completion of the prototype I involved myself in the creative process and began exploring puzzle designs and game play concepts. The prototype was shown to the company committee who received it favorably and green lit its development. While the game's primary focus was always on puzzles, various other features, such as minigames and RPG elements, were explored for game play purposes.

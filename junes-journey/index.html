June's Journey

Designer, Wooga, Berlin, Germany - 8/16 to 7/20

The Project

June's Journey features hidden object gameplay with an island decorating meta game. It is the spiritual successor to Pearl's Peril.

I joined the team in the lead up to soft launch, coming from Bubble Island 2.

Originally, design responsibilities were split between the ownership of weekly chapter releases and feature work. Later, I shifted to focusing on feature work.

Beyond the feature and content work, my other main area of responsibility were the monthly seasonal decorations and evergreen decorations.
